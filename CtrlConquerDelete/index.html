The goal of the game is to capture the Enemy's castle.
Your castle gains 2 gold every turn.

You can use your gold to buy:
One square, allowing you to expand. (2g)
(Enemy tiles cost 8g)

Farms, which make 3 gold every turn. (15g)
Archers, who protect adjacent tiles. (xg)
Soldiers, who attack enemy towers. (xg)

Mines are tiles that make more money per turn, but cannot be built and must be captured
